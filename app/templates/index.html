Is this site safe?

Yes! This site was made for the community to help allow everybody keep track of their rewards from Clu! It's hard to tell just how much you are gaining if you just look at your wallet every once in a while. Signing in with your wallet does 3 things: Signs a Message to prove you are the owner of your wallet, Stores your wallet address in a database to create an account to start tracking balances, and stores a JWT token based off your user to keep you logged in.

You will NEVER receive a request to send CLU/BNB/Any Token to Anywhere. If you ever get a request to send funds, reject it and immediately contact me on Discord (@macs#0420) or Twitter (@maxbridgland). All source code that this site runs on is available for public viewing and auditing on GitHub. Click the button above to head over to the GitHub Source.



Tips to Stay Safe

• Make sure the URL is https://clutrack.io
• Never give your 12 Phrase Passcode Key to Anybody EVER
• Never give your private key and password to Anybody EVER
• This site will never ask you for your money. If it does check the URL
